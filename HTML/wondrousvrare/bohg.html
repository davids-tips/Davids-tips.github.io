Boots of the High Ground

Wonderous Item-Very Rare

These boots grant advantage on checks to leap over dangerous terrain.In addition, these Boots can be used to turn combat in your favor. Once per day, you can speak the boot’s command phrase (“It’s over, I have the high ground!”) as a bonus action. When this happens, the ground underneath you rises a few feet for one minute and your performance is impeccable.

High Ground

While on the high ground, you gain advantage against any creatures you attack with a melee weapon. Additionally, when a creature moves within 5 feet of you, you can use a reaction to make a single melee attack against them. Once a minute passes or you step off of the high ground, it resumes its normal form.
