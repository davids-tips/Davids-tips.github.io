Rope of Durability

Wonderous Item-Common

This rope has 10 hp and it regenerates 1d10 of them each dawn. Once it loses all of its hp or is burst with a DC 22 Strength check, the rope snaps and is rendered nonmagical. It has resistance to piercing damage and vulnerability to fire damage.
