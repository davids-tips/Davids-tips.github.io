Glove of Sparks

Glove of Sparls

Wonderous Item-Common

The wearer of this leather glove can snap their fingers and ignite a small, candle-like flame in their hand. This flame sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for another 5 feet. The flame persists as long as the wearer holds the glove in a 'snapping' position or until it is blown out.
