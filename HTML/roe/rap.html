Ring of Astral Projection

Attunement to the Astral Plane grants you the ability to project yourself and your allies into that plane. This ring has 8 charges. You can expend one charge from the ring to cast astral projection, but targeting only yourself. For each additional charge you expend at the time of casting, you can affect one additional willing creature. Daily at dawn, roll a d20. On a 20, the ring regains 1 charge.
