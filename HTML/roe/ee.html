Ring of Elemental Earth

Ring, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)

Attunement to the Elemental Plane of Earth causes your skin to become hardened like stone. While attuned to this ring you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ You gain resistance to non-magical piercing and slashing damage not made with adamantine weapons.
    ◾ You can use a bonus action to gain a burrowing speed equal to your walking speed for 1 minute. Once you use this property of the ring, it cannot be used again until the next dawn.
    ◾ When you attack an object or structure with an unarmed attack or with a weapon that deals bludgeoning damage, you deal double damage.
