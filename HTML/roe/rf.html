Ring of Elemental Fey

Ring, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)

Attunement to the potent faerie magic of the Feywild causes your facial features to change slightly to more closely resemble a fey creature. Your eyes become slightly brighter and your ears become slightly longer. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ You can cast the cantrip dancing lights as an action. Your spellcasting ability for this spell is Charisma.
    ◾ You gain advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep. If you already possess the Fey trait, you become immune to being charmed.
    ◾ Due to your connection to the time-warping qualities of the Feywild, you gain more benefits from taking a long rest. Instead of regaining half of your maximum amount of hit dice upon completing a long rest, you instead regain all of your hit dice.
    ◾ As a bonus action, you can teleport to a space you can see within 30 feet. Once you use this property of the ring, you cannot use it again until you have completed a short or long rest.
    ◾ You gain proficiency in Animal Handling checks. If you are already proficient in Animal Handling, your proficiency bonus is doubled for Animal Handling checks.
