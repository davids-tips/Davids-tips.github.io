Ring of Elemental Chaos

Attunement to the Elemental Chaos grants you the ability to draw upon the elemental forces in unexpected ways. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ Upon completing a long rest, roll a d6. You gain a damage resistance as determined by the Elemental Chaos table. This resistance remains until the end of your next long rest.
    ◾ When you hit a creature with an attack, that creature takes an additional 1d8 damage of the type determined by your daily roll on the Elemental Chaos table.
    ◾ When you roll a natural 1 on an attack roll, the elemental damage granted to your attacks backfires, and you take 1d8 damage of the type determined by your daily roll on the Elemental Chaos table. This damage bypasses any resistance or immunity you have to that damage type.
