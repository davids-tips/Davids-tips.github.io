Ring of Elemental Air

Ring, Very Rare (Requires Attunement

Attunement to the Elemental Plane of Air gives you the ability to control the flow of air immediately around you. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ When you fall, you can use your reaction to cause a strong blast of air to burst from the ring and slow your descent, reducing the damage by up to 30 points. If you reduce the damage to 0, you automatically land on your feet. Once you use this property of the ring, it cannot be used again until you have completed a short or long rest.
    ◾You become naturally acclimated to high altitudes, including elevations above 20,000 feet.
    ◾You can use a bonus action to gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed for 1 minute. Once you use this property of the ring, it cannot be used again until the next dawn.
    ◾You can use an action to breathe fresh air directly from the ring, allowing you to take breaths while submerged in liquid, avoid using a limited supply of air in an enclosed environment, or avoid the negative effects of breathing air that would otherwise cause a poisonous or other toxic effect. You must use an action to breathe from the ring every time you wish to stop holding your breath in such an environment.
    ◾You can use an action to create up to a moderate wind in a 20 foot cone emanating from the ring in any direction you choose. The effect lasts for 1 round.
