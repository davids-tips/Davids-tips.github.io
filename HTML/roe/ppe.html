Ring of Positive Planar Evergy

Attunement to the Positive Plane imbues you with a sense of well being and good health. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ You gain resistance to radiant damage.
    ◾ When you roll hit dice at the end of a short rest, you automatically regain the maximum number of hit points from the first hit die you expend.
    ◾ As an action, you can gain the effects of the spell beacon of hope, requiring no concentration, and targeting only yourself, for 1 minute. Once you use this property of the ring, you cannot use it again until the next dawn.
