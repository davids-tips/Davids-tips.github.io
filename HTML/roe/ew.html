Ring of Elemental Water

Ring, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)

Attunment Benefits

While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ You gain resistance to cold damage
    ◾ You can breathe underwater
    ◾ You gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed
    ◾ You become naturally acclimated to extreme cold environments and frigid water, succeeding automatically on saving throws to avoid exhaustion due to exposure to such environments.
    ◾ You can use a bonus action to cause a powerful burst of water to erupt from the ring and strike the creature when you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack on your turn. If the creature is large or smaller, you can either push it back 10 feet or knock it prone. If the creature is huge or larger, it must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 5 feet. Once you use this property of the ring, it cannot be used again until you have completed a short or long rest.
    ◾ As an action, you can cause the ring to create a steady flow of fresh, clean water. You can create up to 5 gallons of water daily with this ability.
