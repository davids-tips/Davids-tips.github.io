Ring of Negative Planar Evergy

Attunement to the Negative Plane gives you an unnatural sense of longevity and vitality. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits:

    ◾ You gain resistance to necrotic damage.
    ◾ You gain advantage on death saving throws.
    ◾ You can cast the spell vampiric touch at its lowest level from the ring, requiring no concentration. Your spellcasting ability for casting this spell from the ring is Intelligence. Once you cast the spell in this way, you cannot cast the spell from the ring again until the next dawn.
