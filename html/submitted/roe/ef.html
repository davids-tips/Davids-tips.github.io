Ring of Elemental Fire
Ring, Very Rare (requires attunement) Attunement to the Elemental Plane of Fire causes your body to become hot to the touch, and your hands to glow a dull red color. While attuned to this ring, you gain the following benefits: You gain resistance to fire damage, you become naturally acclimated to intense heat, and you succeed automatically on saving throws to avoid exhaustion due to exposure to extremely hot environments, as an action, you can cause intense heat to emanate from your hands, allowing you to quickly melt non-magical ice, boil a pot of water, cause a small metal object to become scalding hot, or ignite a readily flammable object (such as a torch, piece of paper, or small campfire) with just a touch, your unarmed strikes deal an additional 1d4 fire damage, and when you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack on your turn, you can use a bonus action to cause that creature to become lit on fire. The affected creature takes 1d6 fire damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature can end this damage by using its action to make a DC 10 Dexterity check to extinguish the flames. Once you use this property of the ring, it cannot be used again until you complete a short or long rest.
